How to Play Rock, Paper, Scissors

Welcome to the Rock, Paper, Scissors Game! Here's how it works:

  1. Choose Your Move: Click on one of the three buttons: Rock ✊, Paper ✋, or Scissors ✌️.
  2. The Computer Makes a Move: The computer will randomly select a move.
  3. See the Result: The game will compare your move with the computer's move.
  4. Scoreboard Update: The scoreboard will be updated with wins and losses.
  5. Play Again: After each round, you can continue playing!
